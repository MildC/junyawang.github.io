Momentus is seeking a top performing, energetic Inside Sales Representative with direct sales experience to join our talented team. As an enthusiastic member of the sales team, you will work in a hunting capacity to increase our client base by managing a complete, complex sales cycle. Reporting to the Director of Business Development, you will be selling our exciting portfolio of cutting-edge mobile products to a green field of nearly endless potential customers. You will be joining a fast moving start-up that offers exceptional growth potential while gaining experience selling to a variety of industries.

Responsibility

  • Identify and acquire new customers through cold calling targeted sales lists, networking and referrals
  • Conduct exceptional Webex presentations and product demos while effectively communicating the competitive advantages and value proposition of our mobile solutions.
  • Deliver consistent sales results while meeting and exceeding sales targets
  • Maintain relationships with existing customers by owning the customer relationship and addressing all other types of requests
  • Represent Momentus at key tradeshow industry events

Qualification

  • 2-3 years of inside sales experience (preferably B2B in a start-up environment) with a proven track record of exceeding your sales targets
  • Knowledge, understanding and use of proven sales concepts
  • High motivation and strong desire to succeed
  • Strong comprehension and understanding of mobile platforms (Android, iOS, Windows Mobile, Blackberry) and mobile apps
  • Minimum Bachelor of Business Administration or Commerce, OR post-secondary credential in business specializing in business OR the equivalent in education and experience
  • Infectious personality, excellent communication skills and great sense of humour will help you fit into our corporate culture

About us

Momentus Software works with leading brands and companies to help bring their business to mobile devices. We’ve had tremendous success in developing Android, iOS applications for Tier-1 customers and our clients include US Fortune 500 companies and some of the hottest start-ups in the Silicon Valley. We are a global company operating out of Vancouver (HQ) with regional offices in San Francisco and Auckland. Our expert Android / iOS developers craft professionally made mobile experiences for consumer and enterprise, while delivering unmatched service and results. Together, our young team brings to your table, over 40 years of mobile industry experience – at tech giants like Adobe Systems, Samsung, Google, Apple and Motorola.
