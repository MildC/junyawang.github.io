Our Story

Founded in October 2012, Momentus Software is a Vancouver-based startup specializing in building next-gen apps for mobile and tablet devices. We are your one-stop development shop for mobile development with in-house expertise in User Interface/Experience, Graphics, Webkit Optimization, Embedded Technology, Mobile and Enterprise Security, Analytics and Advertising Integration. Here at Momentus, we work with a hand-picked team of brilliant engineers who share our passion for mobile technology. Together, our young team brings to your table over 40 years of mobile industry experience alone – at biggies like Adobe Systems, Samsung, Google, Apple, Motorola and startups alike.

Momentus owes every product and project to its teams. The Principals have over 40 years of pure mobile experience combined. But more importantly they and Team-Members have worked with each other spanning multiple companies – counting names like Adobe Systems, Google, Apple, NOOK and smaller Silicon Valley startups alike.

Momentus Software is based in Vancouver, BC. with development teams based across Vancouver, Silicon Valley and Auckland.
