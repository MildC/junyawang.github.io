Dr.Martens Magazine Spread Design

Graphic Design (magazine)

Magaine spreads design for a project of my graphic design course.

The theme I choose was Dr.Martens, and the target audience is the youth who loves fashion.

My Role: Graphic Design

Software used: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign

Developed in October 2011

Story Behind Project

This is my first time doing magazine design and applying grid system in graphic design. I learned different functions of serif and san-serif font. I also leared how important research is, because a designer has to know the style of company you are designing for before start designing for them.

Note*: Both articles are searched online, with names of Introduction of Dr. Martens and The Art of Industrial Manufacture respectively.

Design Process

I start the project with doing researches about information and images online. After researching, I decided to use yellow, black and white as color palette not only because they have high contrast and longstanding, but also due to my research about their style as a combination of fashion and classic after brosing Dr. Martens official website.

I chose san-serif typography as titles, and serif for paragraph texts because of combination of popular and classic styles and different readability.

Then I applied grid system and fit the texts and images I found online into grids. At last, I did minor adjustment based on aesthetics.
