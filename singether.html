Description

Software prototype for the final project of my interface design course, done in collaboration with other 4 members.

The objective was to create an interactive and entertaining online karaoke application for linking remote family members and friends.

Story Behind Project

At first, our team brainstormed about 3 ideas of an ideal application for us to make as a final project, and Singether was the one I came up with. After analyzing every possibilities, we decided to take Singether as our final application and further develop for it.

During development, my responsibility were to decide what will the next interface be when clicking a button and to balance between functional usability and fluent user experience, as well as doing graphic design for icons, buttons and the interface.

This project is meaningful to me not only because of the interface we developed, the idea of online karaoke application also makes me proud.
