clipO

UI/UX Design, Prototype, Motion Graphics

clipO is a concept that we develop for the "Innovation Prize" in Purdue University, sponsored by Schurz Communications and Purdue Universityâ€™s entrepreneurial hub The Foundry. It is a mobile application that aims to help video fans to produce high-quality videos with multiple perspectives, transition effects, and soundtrack using simple devices. It connects users who are interested in the same event or subject. By building connections, users will be able to share their perspectives, have access to more video resources, to edit and then improve their work.

Role: UI/UX designer, Prototype, Motion Graphic

Tools: Illustrator, Photoshop, Edge Animate, After Effects

Team of 3

Developed in April 2014, within 1 month

Process and Outcome

The goal of the contest was to design a concept where technology can benefit our everyday life. We had the option to reinvent an existing technology or create our own design. Our team decided to design an application that will change the way which people used to exchange personal information. In this project, I was responsible for creating wireframes and mockups for the application. I also designed the icon of the application based on our concept and the features of our application. As a result, our project was nominated as one of the final five teams in the competition.
