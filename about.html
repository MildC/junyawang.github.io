about me.

Hi, my name is Junya Wang and I am a senior international student in School of Interactive Arts and Technology (SIAT) at Simon Fraser University. SIAT is an interdisciplinary future-focused school for technologists, artists and designers to learn about interactive design, incorporating aesthetics with technical skills. Studying in SIAT provides me an opportunity to go through various project work, gain experience mainly in graphic, interface and interaction design.

Being professional on designing, programming and applying design theories simutaneously, I can be a combination of graphic, interface and interaction designer. Being creative, having a strong sense of color and taste on style, I am good at designing and uniting the style of project. Being patient, responsible and good at precision, even details of the project needed to be done fastidiously. With strong technical skills, comprehension and organization skill, I can help the team solve problems efficiently.
